What it checks

Every angle of fit, before you hit apply.

Seven checks run automatically the moment you open a job listing — no uploads, no waiting room.

01 / SCORE

Job Match Score

Understand how closely your resume matches the selected job, expressed as a single clear percentage.

02 / ATS

ATS Compatibility Check

Detect formatting and parsing issues before a recruiter — or the bot before them — ever sees your resume.

03 / KEYWORDS

Missing Keywords

Find the important terms from the job description that are missing from your resume entirely.

04 / SKILLS

Skill Gap Analysis

Identify the specific skills the role requires that your current resume doesn't yet demonstrate.

05 / STRENGTHS

Resume Strengths

See what already makes your profile competitive for this exact role, not just resumes in general.

06 / SUGGEST

Improvement Suggestions

Receive specific, actionable AI recommendations you can act on in minutes, not vague advice.

07 / ONE-CLICK

One Click Analysis

Analyze any job directly from LinkedIn, YC Jobs, Wellfound, Naukri, and more — no copy-pasting.
